Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

GridSpace ("the App") is developed by GridSpace Apps. This privacy policy describes what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. The following data is collected automatically:

Analytics Data

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the App is used. This includes:

  • App usage events (e.g., puzzles started, puzzles completed, difficulty selected)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • General crash and performance data

This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

Crash Reports

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports. These include device state information at the time of a crash, which helps us fix bugs and improve stability. Crash reports do not contain personal information.

Advertising

The App displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use the following:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA), if you grant tracking permission
  • General device information (device model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, taps)

If you decline the App Tracking Transparency prompt, AdMob will serve contextual (non-personalized) ads and will not access your advertising identifier.

For more information on how Google handles data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect or have access to your payment information.

Data Storage

Your puzzle progress and game saves are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, sync, or store your game data on any server.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • Ad tracking: You can control personalized advertising through the App Tracking Transparency prompt, or in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.
  • Analytics: You can limit ad tracking and analytics data collection through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
